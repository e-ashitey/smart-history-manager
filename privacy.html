Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2026

Introduction

Smart History Manager respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data the extension accesses and how it is used.

Data Collection

Smart History Manager processes all browsing history locally on your device. The extension does not collect, transmit, or store any of your browsing data externally.

Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions for its functionality:

  • history – to read and delete browsing history locally.
  • storage – to store user preferences such as domain overrides and ignored sessions.
  • tabs – reserved for optional future features; no tracking is performed.

How Data is Used

All processing happens on your device to allow you to:

  • Search and group browsing history by keyword or URL.
  • Review browsing sessions and selectively delete personal history.
  • Store your preferences for domains marked as Work or Personal.

Third-Party Sharing

No data is shared with us or any third party. Your browsing history never leaves your device.
